Services tailored for seniors

Whether you need a quick tune-up or ongoing coaching, JB the Tech Guy offers calm, reliable help with all of your everyday technology.

Computer Repair & Upgrades

Speed up slow computers, install memory or storage, and keep Windows or Mac systems healthy with regular checkups.

Personalized Lessons

One-on-one instruction at your pace for email, photo sharing, online safety, and everyday tasks that matter to you.

Phones & Tablets

Get help choosing the right device, setting it up, managing contacts, and creating simple safety plans for staying connected.

TVs, Audio & Streaming

Install and adjust TVs, remotes, and speakers, organize streaming services, and make sure captions and accessibility settings are easy to use.

Internet & Wi-Fi

Review cable or internet bills, suggest money-saving plans, improve Wi-Fi coverage, and secure your network for peace of mind.

Smart Home Safety

Install and support cameras, doorbells, and smart alerts so you can see who is at the door and keep family members informed.
